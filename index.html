--- layout: default title: Itai Bassi --- I am a theoretical linguist. My research interests, broadly speaking, are in semantics and its interfaces with syntax and pragmatics. I have published work in a number of empirical domains, including: Scalar Implicatures, anaphora and binding, focus and ellipsis.

I graduated from the department of linguistics at MIT in September 2021, and now I am a post-doctoral researcher in the LeibnizDream project at the Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft (ZAS), Berlin.
