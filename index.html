--- layout: default title: Itai Bassi --- I am a theoretical linguist. My research interests, broadly speaking, are in semantics and its interfaces with syntax and pragmatics. I have published work in a number of empirical domains, including: Scalar Implicatures, anaphora and binding, focus and ellipsis. Here is my CV (January 2025).

I graduated from the department of linguistics at MIT in September 2021. I am now a post-doctoral researcher in the LeibnizDream project at the Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft (ZAS), and at Ben Gurion University.

Starting November 2024, I am co-editor of the journal Snippets together with Gary Thoms.
